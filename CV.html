JEREMÍAS MARÍN

GRAPHIC DESIGNER

My name is Jeremias, I am located in General Roca, Argentina. I love design and visual art in every shape and form. I worked in design, mostly packaging and advertising, printing, preparing files and rendering. Lately I started to do UX and coding to expand my horizons and I am really enjoying this and want to learn more about it.

SKILLS

Adobe Illustrator / Adobe Photoshop / Figma / Adobe XD / HTML / CSS / Procreate / Cinema 4d / Adobe InDesign / Adobe After Effects

LANGUAJES

Spanish native / English conversational

WORK EXPERIENCE

• FREELANCE DESIGN WORKING / 2010-present Description: Various design jobs for independent contractors.

• CASAVERDE SCHOOL / 2014-present Description: Academic Secretary, mixed tasks.

• UDEMY / 2014-2015 Description: Cover design for online courses.

• YAÑEZ GIOIA DESIGN / 2012-2014 Description: 3d render and file printing optimization (offset and flexography).

• OESTE ARGENTINO S.R.L. / 2010-2011 Description: File printing optimization (offset and flexography) and production and quality coordination.

• BROKERS (ADVERTISING AND MARKETING) / 2009-2010 Description: Branding, promotion and commercial pieces for the Vea Supermarket (national reach supermarket chain).

EDUCATION

• GRAPHIC DESIGN / 2003-2012 National University of Cuyo - Arts and Design Faculty

• UX/UI Design / 2021 Coderhouse
